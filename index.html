Pasta Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a separate pan, cook pancetta until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Drain spaghetti and add to the pan with pancetta.
  5. Quickly stir in egg mixture until coated.
  6. Season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.

Classic Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough ball
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out pizza dough and place on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add mozzarella cheese and basil leaves.
  5. Drizzle olive oil over the pizza.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove from oven, slice, and serve hot.
